Computer has a 4-digit secret number. The digits are all different. 
You have to guess this number. Computer gives the number of matches. 
If the matching digits are in their right positions, they are "bulls", 
if in different positions, they are "cows". 
Example:    
Secret number: 4271
Your try: 1234
Answer: 1 bull and 2 cows. (The bull is "2", the cows are "4" and "1".)
